## Ask Rishi

**Label:** Ask Rishi

**Headline:** Ask anything about your customers. Get a clear answer in seconds.

**Sub headline:** Your AI research analyst — always on, always ready.

**Tagline:** From question to insight in minutes — no waiting, no digging.

**Body:**
Rishi understands every format your customers respond in — text, video, voice, and more — and synthesises them into one clear answer.

Across languages, channels, and studies — Rishi connects the signals that matter most to your team.

**Features:**
- Understands context across multiple studies
- Surfaces themes, not just statistics
- Explains its reasoning, not just its findings
- Recommends next actions based on evidence

**Example queries & responses:**

Query 1: "What are the top concerns from our NPS survey this month?"
→ I analyzed 847 responses from your Q2 NPS study. Three themes are driving your detractor scores:
  - Onboarding Complexity (34%) — "The setup felt overwhelming. I needed more guidance at the start."
  - Feature Discoverability (28%) — "I didn't know half these features existed until a colleague told me."
  - Support Response Time (21%) — "For a paid product, response times were slower than I expected."
  Recommendation: Onboarding is your highest-leverage fix. It affects new users in their first 30 days and correlates with 2× churn risk in months 2–3.

Query 2: "Which customer segment is most at risk of churning right now?"
→ Cross-referencing your last 3 studies, the highest churn risk profile is:
  - SMB accounts under 90 days old (62%) — Low activation score. No team members added.
  - Users who skipped onboarding (47%) — Never completed their first study.
  - Single-seat Growth plan accounts (38%) — High initial activity then sharp drop-off after week 3.
  Recommendation: Trigger a targeted check-in study for criteria 1 and 2. Estimated recovery potential: 18% of at-risk monthly revenue.

Query 3: "How has sentiment shifted since last quarter?"
→ Comparing Q1 and Q2 across 1,240 responses:
  - Positive sentiment: 58% (up from 44%) — new UI and faster load times are being noticed.
  - Neutral / mixed: 28% — stable, clusters around pricing clarity.
  - Negative: 14% (down from 26%) — support investment in Q1 showing results.
  Recommendation: Trend is strongly positive. Remaining negatives concentrated in enterprise accounts.
